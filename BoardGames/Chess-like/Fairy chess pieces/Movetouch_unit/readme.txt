A move-touch/proximity-cast unit
applies an effect/spell on units
that touch its movement squares.
Example:
Unit A moves from a1 to a8,
and enemy unit at b5 is now under
a 'move-touch' spell X.

Variations:
1.Paralysis touch:
Units being touched by move
are paralysed for N moves.
2.Capture touch:
The first N units touched
will be captured.
3.Push-touch:
units being touched are pushed
out of movement line direction.
4.Pull-touch: units touched
are pulled into nearest move-path
 square.
5.Morph touch:
First N units touched are set under
 morpth spell.
6.Conversion touch:
A conversion spell is cast towards
first N touched units.
