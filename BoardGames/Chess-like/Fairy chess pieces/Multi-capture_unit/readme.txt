A multi-capture unit is capable
of capturing multiple pieces
in its attack range, provided
they don't block each other.
e.g. knight capturing both ends of
a fork from a3 to b1 and c2, moving
to c2 or b1 (as player decides).

Types:
1.gained ability:
The multicapture unit gains
its power from some game event
e.g. capturing another piece gains
+1 extra capture per move.
2.Innate ability:
The unit has this ability
from piece-type ability.
3.Limited-used ability:
The unit has only N multicaptures
 per game(typically one),
