Also named hidden/stasis units:
such units can enter a hidden/burrowed
 state where they cannot move but
 cannot be targeted(except some spells).
 
Types:
1.Burrowing:
The unit is 'place underground'
 existing below a square, which
 can be occupied by pther units.
The unit cannot be targeted before
unburrowing,which requires the square
occupied being free of other units.

2.Hidden by spell:
Unit is placed into a state
similar to Shadow units, except
it cannot move or use abilities.
Moving into the square
of unit is usually allowed.

3.Stasis:
Unit is placed into stasis
by proximity to some other unit
or spell area, which prevents its capture
Movement into the square is prevented.
