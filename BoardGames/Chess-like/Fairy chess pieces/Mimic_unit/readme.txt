A mimic unit can use powers or
movement type of any unit
that can target it.

Types:
1.Move mimic:
This unit can move as
any unit that can target it(capture
 range),allowing it to capture
 the mimicked piece.
 
2.Ability steal:
This type unit also has
the powers/abilities of any piece
that is capable of targeting it.

3.Reflecting:
This type of unit reflects the
 ability/move back to its target,
 after the capture/ability is used.
 e.g. removing its own capturer from
 board or casting back a spell towards
 its spellcaster.
