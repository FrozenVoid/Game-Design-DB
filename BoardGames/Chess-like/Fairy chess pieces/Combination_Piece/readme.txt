A combination piece is a hybrid
 unit containing 2+ pieces,
 which result from combination
  move(e.g. Knight moving into allied Bishop:
  this creates a KnightBishop combination piece).
  
  Limitations & rules:
 
 1.Total max of combo pieces per player:
  e.g. 4 combination pieces.
  
  2.Merging of pieces is usually
  permanent, but can be made
  temporary with unmberging moves
  out of combination piece square.
