A shooter unit(e.g. cannon)
has a separate range of attack and range
of movement(usually very limited) with
its capture type being a stationary
 cast to square ability(shooting).

The shooting unit attack
is usually counte-balanced by cooldown
(e.g. cannon can shoot once per 3 moves)
limited shots per square(e.g. once per square)
limited ammo(N shots per cannon per game)
or various other rules(e.g.
 cannot shoot at same square again/
cannot target specific type of units
or cannot shoot targets too close)
