Egg spawning is the ability 
of piece-type or spell cast
on unit which produces the egg
at unoccupied,safe square(untargeted
by enemy or area spell)
of player choice in 3x3 grid 
center on egg-production unit.

Variants:
1.Innate ability:
e.g. dragon able to spawn eggs
at some cooldown cost(N player moves).

2.Spellcast:
Spellcaster unit cast 'Egg spawn'
on mundane unit to produce an 
egg at some cooldown cost per spell.

3.Enemy spell:
Enemy spawns own egg(s) of player's unit
piece-type,though this is rarely
useful due eggs easily being captured
 in such case.
