A portal unit is a movable
passive piece that allows
units stepping into its square
to teleport to the destination
square as if it was a portal.
Types:
1.Proximity portal:
Portal unit destination square
is variable range of its 'attack range'.
selected destination is chosen
after stepping into the portal
(occupied) and can be delayed
as if the portal was a unit-cannon.

2.Start-square portal:
the portal unit last move start
square is the portal and its current
location is the destination.

3.Portal pair:
Unit A and Unit B  are linked
portals which transport pieces
stepping into A to B and B to A.

4.Manual cast:
The portal unit cast a limited
duration/use portal destination
(that can be destroyed or used),
which is the arrival square
of pieces stepping into portal square.
