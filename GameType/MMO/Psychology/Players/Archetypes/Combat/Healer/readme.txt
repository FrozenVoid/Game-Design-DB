Healer archetype usually utilizes spells or items to heal
allied players in combat, maintain buffs and debuffs in area 
while staying outside close combat zone;
Damage-oriented builds usually don't invest as much
in healing will benefit from such specialized build.

Healing is the main specialization of such archetype;
direct spells, healing projectiles, healing auras and artifacts 
which add 'healing/regen',
as well as potion making are primary skills.

Buffs that healers provide can be classified as 
A.Protection buff; add anti-curse/anti-debuff status effects.
B.Status removal buffs; removing debuffs/spell effects in area.
C.Attribute Boosting buff;e.g.regeneration buff.
specialization on A,B or C determine the archetype subtype.
