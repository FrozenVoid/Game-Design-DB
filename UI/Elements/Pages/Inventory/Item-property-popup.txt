Item property popups allow editing or examing item properties,
by click the item property lines in tooltip/page of selected item;
examples:
1.item personalization(e.g. click to name this item)
2.inscriptions(e.g. add magic word to item(similar to insertables))
3.insertables(e.g. remove/replace/add insertable object)
4.text message sub-objects(e.g. adding descriptions or messages)
5.material sheet(examine materials to which item can be disassembled)
6.item worth stats(e.g. marketplace average/vendor price).
