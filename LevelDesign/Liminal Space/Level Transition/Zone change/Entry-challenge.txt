Entry challenge are a form of gating levels with 
various quest-like setups, with player permanent entry to a zone
dependent on completing a challenge in entry area.
Typical challenge:
1.acrobatic puzzle; usually a sequence of jumps to reach key object.
2.Maze solving: a small maze with traps or moving elements(sokoban-type).
3.Mini-game with NPC; usually a short game of skill
4.Mini-game with area objects:usually  Placement/Configuration puzzles.
5.Trick attack: players have to perform some skill with precision.
