Area handicaps are temporary debuffs that either disable or nullify 
some player attribute for additional challenge.
Types of handicaps:
1.Spell class handicap: some class of spells is disabled.
2.item attribute depower;specific item attributes cease to work
or greatly diminished in power/scope.
3.regen handicap; stats/charges cannot be restored by attribute.
4.Speed handicap; area limits all terrain speeds.
5.Vision handicap; area is obscured by e.g. fog.
