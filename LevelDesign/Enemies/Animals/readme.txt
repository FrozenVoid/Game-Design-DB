List of animal monster archetypes:
Ravens(staring/sitting on objects)
Rats(moving in background, in the walls, in objects, falling rats,etc)
Black cats/dogs/etc in low light
Bats(colonies, sudden vertical attacks)
Snakes(nests, moving from small holes)
Owls(flying and screaming)
Larger than usual Insects/invertebrates(nests, hives, tunnels,confined spaces).
Animal-Human mixes: hybrids/chimeras.
Mutants: animals with deformities.
